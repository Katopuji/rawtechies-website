Who We Are
Built Raw.
Built Real.

RawTechies is a Sri Lanka-based digital company with one mission — to build platforms that truly serve Sri Lankans in their daily lives.

We believe the Sri Lankan internet user deserves fast, relevant, local-first content — from breaking news and live exchange rates to free education and classifieds.

We are a lean, focused team that moves fast, builds bold, and delivers real value to real people. No corporate fluff. Just raw, honest digital work.
