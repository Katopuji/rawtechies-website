Who We Are
Built Raw.
Built Real.

RawTechies is a Sri Lanka-based digital media company with one mission — to build platforms that truly serve Sri Lankans in their daily lives.

We believe the Sri Lankan internet user deserves fast, relevant, Sinhala-first content — from breaking news and live exchange rates to job listings, health information, and real estate.

We are a lean, focused team that moves fast, builds bold, and delivers real value to real people. No corporate fluff. Just raw, honest digital work.

Why We Exist
01

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's most trusted and widely used digital media network — accessible to every Sri Lankan, in every district, on every device.

02

Our Mission

To build Sinhala-first digital platforms that connect Sri Lankans with the news, jobs, finance, health, and services they need — every single day.
