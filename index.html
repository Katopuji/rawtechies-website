Who We Are
Built Raw.
Built Real.

RawTechies is a Sri Lanka-based digital company with one mission — to build platforms that truly serve Sri Lankans in their daily lives.

We believe the Sri Lankan internet user deserves fast, relevant, Sinhala-first content — from breaking news and live exchange rates to free education for every child, regardless of where they live.

We are a lean team that moves fast, builds bold, and delivers real value to real people. No corporate fluff. No investors to please. Just raw, honest digital work — built in Sri Lanka, for Sri Lanka.

The best technology is the kind that disappears — it just works, and people's lives get better because of it.

The Founder
The Person Behind It
M
Mohamed
Founder & Builder · RawTechies

Based in Sri Lanka. Solo founder and builder behind all three RawTechies platforms. Started with a simple question — what does Sri Lanka actually need from the internet? — and has been building the answer ever since. Driven by the belief that technology should close gaps, not create them. Working to give every Sri Lankan child, farmer, and daily wage earner the same access to information that Colombo residents take for granted.

Why We Exist

Our Vision
